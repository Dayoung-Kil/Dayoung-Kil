### 🧑‍💻 About Me

I'm a Ph.D. student at the **Visual Intelligence and Platform (VIP) Lab**,
Soongsil University.

My work lives at the **accuracy–compute frontier**: how far can large vision
and multimodal models be pushed under real compute and latency budgets without
giving up capability? I currently focus on **token pruning and efficient
inference for Large Vision-Language Models (LVLMs)**, extending my earlier work
in structured network pruning and lightweight visual perception.

- 🔭 Currently working on efficient inference for **LVLMs / Multimodal LLMs**
- 🌱 Foundations in **structured pruning** & **lightweight architectures**
- 💬 Happy to chat about anything in **efficient deep learning**
